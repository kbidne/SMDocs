## üí° Three Truths About Design + AI

### Truth 1: Speed Isn't Enough

Anyone can ask Midjourney for "a button." You know that won't work.

But **you** can:
- Interview 5 users about what they need
- Understand their context
- Make intentional choices
- Validate with real feedback
- Explain why something works

**Speed + intentionality beats speed alone.**

### Truth 2: Design Is About Decisions, Not Pixels

```
‚ùå "I designed this button"
   (Meaning: I moved pixels around)

‚úÖ "I decided this button should be this size because users
   struggle at smaller sizes, and our accessibility
   standard requires 44x44px minimum"
   (Meaning: I made an informed choice)
```

AI can move pixels. Only you can make decisions.

### Truth 3: Documentation IS Design Work

```
You spend 5 minutes documenting why you chose something.

That 5 minutes:
- Prevents a developer spending 30 minutes re-designing it
- Allows a new designer to learn from your thinking
- Proves your value to stakeholders
- Creates institutional memory

That's design work. That's valuable.
```
